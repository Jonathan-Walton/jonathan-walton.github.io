Chapter 3 of The Great Gatsby finally introduces the much-anticipated Jay Gatsby. Nick receives an invitation to one of Gatsby's lavish parties. Throughout the party, Nick becomes familiar with the many rumors surrounding Gatsby. The rumors consist of tales involving how Gatsby got his money, some suggest he killed a man or is related to someone in power. He is also introduced to Owl Eyes at this party. Nick eventually does meet Gatsby, and is slightly taken aback how interesting Gatsby is.
