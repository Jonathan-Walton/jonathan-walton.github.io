Chapter 1 of The Great Gatsby is mainly a expository chapter; it introduces most the characters and foreshadows upcoming events. Nick Carraway is the main character, and the in the first chapter, it is explained why he moved to West Egg along with his relationships to Daisy Buchanan, Tom Buchanan, and Jordan Baker.

Setting

In chapter 1, Fitzgerald introduces the reader to two of the main settings of The Great Gatsby - East Egg and West Egg. Named for their resemblence to eggs, these two regions are the ficticious home to the wealthy. However, East Egg is home to the wealthy whom inherited their wealth: old money, while West Egg is home to the newly rich. Various other differences are made between the two eggs as well, like the conservative and classy nature of East Egg as compared to the frivolity and gaudiness of West Egg.
